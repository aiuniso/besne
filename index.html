Movie Information

Release Date: July 18, 2008

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Director: Christopher Nolan

Duration: 152 minutes

Plot: When the menace known as The Joker emerges from his mysterious past, he wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham. The Dark Knight must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Actors

Christian Bale

Role: Bruce Wayne / Batman

Christian Bale is known for his portrayal of Bruce Wayne, a billionaire who fights crime as Batman. His dedication to the role is legendary, having undergone significant physical transformations over the years.

Heath Ledger

Role: The Joker

Heath Ledger's portrayal of The Joker is widely regarded as one of the greatest performances in film history. His chaotic and unpredictable character stole the show in the movie.

Aaron Eckhart

Role: Harvey Dent / Two-Face

Aaron Eckhart plays Harvey Dent, a once-promising district attorney who becomes the tragic villain Two-Face. His transformation from hero to villain is a central arc in the film.
